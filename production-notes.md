There is a later PD translation, Malkin's, but it appears to be obscure. There are no scholarly references online, and a conversation with Alexander Pettit at U. of North Texas revealed he had not heard of it. Much of it appears identical to Smollett's. Thus we are opting for Smollett's translation, even though it's slightly older.
